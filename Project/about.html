How AquaVision AI Works?

AquaVision AI uses a convolutional neural network (CNN) trained on a large dataset of aquarium fish images. When you upload a photo, the AI processes the image through multiple layers to extract features and patterns, ultimately classifying the fish species with high accuracy.
